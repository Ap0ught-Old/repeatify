Repeatify the simplest player to repeat Spotify music

Download

Repeatify is submitted to Mac App Store for approval, you can download the latest release build by clicking the Repeatify icon.

Features

  • Repeat your favorite Spotify tracks by one click
  • Sync your Spotify playlists, inbox and starred playlist
  • Play/Pause control by media key

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Spotify Premium account to use Repeatify?

Yes, please upgrade your Spotify account to Premium to use third-party apps and offline features.

I have an idea for Repeatify, how can I contribute?

Fork Repeatify on Github, and ask for a pull request after your modifications.

How to contact you?

Email, Twitter, or other contacts
